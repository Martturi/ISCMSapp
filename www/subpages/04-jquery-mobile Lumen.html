LUMEN

The title of the Festival is Lumen which means 'Light'. The theme of 'light' is apt as participants will be illuminated and their spirits will shine with the brilliance and energy of their music-making.

The International Schools Choral Music Society's annual Music Festival gives talented music students from all over the world the chance to sing in a large chorus or play in a full sized symphony orchestra. ISCMS students and teachers will have the opportunity to learn from internationally renowned guest artists and practitioners. Students will perform some stunning repertoire and have the chance to attend a range of workshops. Teachers will be offered Professional Development by leading experts.

ISCMS Music Festival 2012 - Lumen will be held in Beijing from

Wednesday 15 February to Sunday 19 February 2012

In the Forbidden City Concert Hall, Beijing the Festival will be hosted by Dulwich College Beijing.

To find out more about the ISCMS Music Festival Beijing 2012 - Lumen, find us at our website.

Repertoire:
Gloria and Te Deum - Karl Jenkins
A selection of gospel music
Lord of the Dance Medley - Ronan Hardiman
West Side Story Medley - Leonard Bernstein
Qing Zhu (Celebration) - Karl Jenkins - newly commissioned work - world premiere
More repertoire to be announced
